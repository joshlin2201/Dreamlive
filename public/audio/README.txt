Place your audio files in this folder!

Supported formats:
- .mp3
- .wav
- .ogg
- .m4a
- .flac

Files will appear in alphabetical order in the dropdown menus.

Example files you might add:
- 01-background-music.mp3
- 02-performance-song-1.mp3
- 03-performance-song-2.mp3
- 04-sound-effect-applause.wav
- 05-intro-music.mp3

Tip: Number your files (01-, 02-, etc.) to control the order they appear!
